Mac Cought Trojan Horse on 06/04/2015

Apr 7th, 2015 8:11 pm

My MacBookPro caught a trojan virus which is disguised as video player on 6th April 2015, I’ve installed some anti-virus softwares, no one works.

And this “[转载] mac wirelurker木马病毒检查！！” - http://bbs.feng.com/read-htm-tid-8560800.html is only for killing ‘wirelurker’ trojan, not for my case.

I searched Google in Chinese “Mac网页木马”，and found the following resource, “Mac也要掃毒/木馬！《Adware removal tool》解決Chrome、Safari 被廣告綁架” http://www.applealmond.com/mac-tutorial/adware-removal-toolchromesafari

When I downloaded the ‘Adware removal tool’, it is already outofdate and gives me a new link http://www.thesafemac.com/art/ directs to a ‘real app’ called ‘AdwareMedic’, the downloaded link is ‘http://www.adwaremedic.com/index.php’, when I installed this one, the unwanted pop-ups stop appearing.

It solved at present
